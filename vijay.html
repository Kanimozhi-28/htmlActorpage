vijay

Actor Vijay, widely known as "Thalapathy" by his fans, is one of the most celebrated actors in the Tamil film industry. Born on June 22, 1974, as Joseph Vijay Chandrasekhar, he is the son of filmmaker S. A. Chandrasekhar and playback singer Shoba Chandrasekhar. Vijay's cinematic journey began as a child actor, and he made his debut as a lead in Naalaiya Theerpu (1992). Over the years, he has become a versatile performer, delivering blockbuster hits like Ghilli, Thuppakki, Mersal, and Master. Known for his electrifying screen presence, captivating dance moves, and action-packed roles, Vijay has amassed a massive fan following worldwide. Apart from his cinematic achievements, he is admired for his philanthropic activities and remains a prominent figure in Tamil Nadu's cultural and social spheres.
